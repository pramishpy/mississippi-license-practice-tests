About the Test

What to Expect

  • 30 multiple-choice questions
  • Need 24 correct answers to pass (80%)
  • Topics cover road signs, traffic laws, and safe driving
  • Computer-based format at your local DMV

How to Prepare

  1. Study the Mississippi Driver's Manual
  2. Take our practice tests regularly
  3. Review missed questions
  4. Focus on road signs and traffic rules
